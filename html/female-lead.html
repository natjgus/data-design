Who Is My Strong Female Lead?

Personality Characteristics:

In my new blockbuster movie SpaceJam III: Nat vs. SuperHeron, I would want a female lead that is strong and sassy. I like to give people a hard time, but this lead actress would have to be able to out-do me in this category. She has a very strong personality and does not like to be wrong. Fortunately, she rarely is wrong since she is always a bit more level-headed than the rest of us.


However, like any person, she is not without her flaws. Sometimes she gets a bit to heated when interacting with the dimwitted MonStars that we must team up with to beat SuperHeron and his super team.

Physical Characteristics:

Raquel (the name of the character) has an interesting background. She is half Brazilian, half Nepalese. She was raised in the Australian Outback and speaks with a STRONG Australian accent. Because of this interesting background, the actress must have a but of a clooudy ethnicity. She will have darker, sun-tanned skin. She is our point guard so is a bit smaller than the rest of the players but very fast. Raquel wears her hair in a loose afro that bounces wildly while she talks. She is very fit but is missing 2 fingers off of her left hand from an incident with a dingo. The most striking thing about Raquel is her piercing blue eyes.
