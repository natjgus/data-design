Data Design of the Medium Website

Persona

  • Name: Kat Fitzgerald
  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 29
  • Technology:
    • Uses MacBook Air and iPhone X
    • Always has iPhone in her hand
    • Lots of interaction on the site
  • Needs: Weekly contributor to the "Culture" side of the website

User Story

As a site contributor, Kat wants to easily upload content and view comments

Use Case

Kat posts her weekly article about the pluses of millenials starting to join the political sphere. 3 hours after publication she realizes that there is a glaring typo that she must fix. Google Docs changed the word "pluses" to "perils." She needs to edit the article without deleting the article and associated comments

Precondition: Kat is a registered user

Postcondition: Article post is updated

Interaction Flow

  • Kat logs in to her Medium profile
  • The site gives Kat a variety of options
  • Kat clicks "Modify Post"
  • The site opens the post with the ability to edit the text
  • Kat fixes her typo that was overlooked by her and the editor
  • The site asks if she would like to update changes
  • Kat clicks "Yes, update changes"
  • The site saves the new, updated version of the article
  • Justin Bieber reads the article and is inspired to run for Governor of Idaho
